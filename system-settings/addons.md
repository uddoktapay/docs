## Introduction

Addons extend UddoktaPay functionality with additional features and integrations. View available addons, manage installed ones, and purchase new capabilities.

Access Addons from **Administration → System Settings → Addons**.

## Available Addons

- Liquid (ResellerCamp)

- Orderbox (ResellerCamp)

- Role Management

## Manage Addon

For installed addons, click **Manage** to access addon-specific configuration.

**What happens:**
- Opens addon settings page
- Configure addon features
- Enable/disable addon functionality
- Adjust addon-specific options

Each addon has its own management interface with unique settings.

## Purchase Addon

For addons not included in your license, click **Purchase** to buy the addon.

**Purchase process:**

1. Click **Purchase** on the addon card
2. Redirects to [my.uddoktapay.com](https://my.uddoktapay.com)
3. Review addon pricing and features
4. Complete purchase
5. Return to panel
6. Go to **Administration → License**
7. Click **Refresh License**
8. Addon appears in Available Addons list
9. Features become accessible

::: tip
After purchasing an addon at [my.uddoktapay.com](https://my.uddoktapay.com), always click **Refresh License** in **Administration → License** to activate the addon in your panel.
:::

## Checking Your Addons

To see which addons are included in your license:

1. Go to **Administration → License**
2. Scroll to **Available Addons** section
3. Compare with Addons page to see what's available

## Best Practices

**Before purchasing:**
- Review addon features and use cases
- Check if addon solves your specific need
- Verify license compatibility
- Review pricing at my.uddoktapay.com

**After installation:**
- Click Manage to configure addon settings
- Read addon-specific documentation
- Test addon features in development first
- Train staff on new addon capabilities

## Troubleshooting

**Addon not appearing after purchase**

Problem: Purchased addon doesn't show in panel.

**Solution:**
1. Verify purchase completed at [my.uddoktapay.com](https://my.uddoktapay.com)
2. Go to **Administration → License**
3. Click **Refresh License**
4. Check **Available Addons** section
5. If still missing, contact support with purchase receipt

**Manage button does nothing**

Problem: Clicking Manage doesn't open settings.

**Solution:**
1. Refresh the page
2. Clear browser cache
3. Try different browser
4. Check browser console for errors
5. Verify addon is properly installed

**Purchase link broken**

Problem: Purchase button doesn't redirect.

**Solution:**
1. Check internet connection
2. Verify [my.uddoktapay.com](https://my.uddoktapay.com) is accessible
3. Try clicking again
4. Copy URL manually and visit in new tab
5. Contact support if link consistently fails

::: warning
Addon availability and features depend on your license type. Some addons may require specific license tiers. Check [my.uddoktapay.com](https://my.uddoktapay.com) for complete addon requirements.
:::
