## Quick Start

1. Configure **[General Settings](/brand-settings/general)** first
2. Upload your **[Logo & Favicon](/brand-settings/logo)**
3. Set up **[Mail Settings](/brand-settings/mail)** for notifications
4. Configure **[API Settings](/brand-settings/api)** for integrations
5. Customize your **[Theme](/brand-settings/themes)**
