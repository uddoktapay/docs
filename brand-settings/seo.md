## Introduction

SEO Settings configure search engine optimization and social media preview settings for your brand. These settings control how your payment pages appear in search results and when shared on social platforms.

Access SEO Settings from **Brand Settings → SEO** to optimize your online visibility and social sharing appearance.

## Meta Description

A brief, compelling description of your business shown in search engine results.

**Where it appears:**
- Google search results (below your site title)
- Bing and other search engines
- Meta description tag in page HTML

**Character limit:**  
Keep under **160 characters** for optimal display in search results.

Longer descriptions may be truncated with "..." in search results.

**Best practices:**
- Summarize what your business offers
- Include primary keywords naturally
- Make it compelling to encourage clicks
- Write for humans, not just search engines
- Avoid keyword stuffing

**Example:**  
"Accept payments online with UddoktaPay. Fast, secure payment processing for businesses in Bangladesh. Multiple payment methods supported."

## SEO Keywords

Keywords representing your business focus and services.

**Format:**  
Comma-separated list of relevant keywords.

**Example:**  
`payments, invoices, uddoktapay, digital wallet, online payments, bangladesh`

**Usage:**
- Helps search engines understand your business
- Improves relevance for search queries
- Supports content categorization

**Selection tips:**
- Include your business name
- Add service-related terms
- Consider location keywords if relevant
- Use terms customers search for
- Avoid unrelated or spam keywords

**Recommended count:**  
5-10 focused keywords work better than 20+ generic ones.

## Social Media Sharing

Configure preview images when your links are shared on social platforms.

### Facebook/LinkedIn Image

Preview image appearing when links shared on Facebook and LinkedIn.

**Recommended size:**  
1200 × 630 pixels

**Where it appears:**
- Facebook post link previews
- LinkedIn shared links
- WhatsApp link previews
- Other platforms using Open Graph protocol

**Upload:**
- Drag & drop or click Browse
- Format: JPG, JPEG, PNG
- High-quality images only

**Design tips:**
- Use your logo and brand colors
- Include minimal text (Facebook may reject text-heavy images)
- Ensure image looks good at thumbnail size
- Test how it appears when shared

### Twitter/X Card Image

Preview image appearing when links shared on Twitter/X.

**Recommended size:**  
1200 × 628 pixels

**Where it appears:**
- Twitter/X link cards
- Tweet link previews

**Upload:**
- Drag & drop or click Browse
- Format: JPG, JPEG, PNG
- High-quality images only

**Design tips:**
- Similar to Facebook image but optimized for Twitter's card format
- Can use same image as Facebook or create Twitter-specific version
- Ensure text is readable at card size
- Preview using Twitter Card Validator

::: tip
Upload high-quality images for professional appearance across all platforms. Blurry or pixelated images negatively impact brand perception.
:::

## Best Practices

**Meta Description:**
- Front-load important keywords
- Include clear call-to-action when appropriate
- Make every character count (stay under 160)
- Avoid duplicate descriptions if managing multiple brands
- Update seasonally or with major changes

**SEO Keywords:**
- Research what customers actually search
- Include variations (singular/plural, synonyms)
- Add location terms if serving specific regions
- Avoid keyword stuffing or irrelevant terms
- Review and update quarterly

**Social Images:**
- Maintain consistent branding across both images
- Use high-resolution source files
- Include your logo for brand recognition
- Test links on actual platforms before launch
- Update images when rebranding

**Testing:**
- Use Google's Rich Results Test for meta tags
- Test Facebook shares with Facebook Sharing Debugger
- Validate Twitter cards with Twitter Card Validator
- Share test links privately before public announcements
- Clear platform caches when updating images

**Optimization:**
- Compress images without losing quality
- Use descriptive alt text when platforms support it
- Ensure images work in both light and dark modes
- Consider accessibility (readable text, good contrast)

**Monitoring:**
- Track click-through rates from search results
- Monitor social engagement on shared links
- A/B test different descriptions and images
- Update based on performance data

::: warning
After uploading or changing social media images, platforms may cache the old version for hours or days. Use platform-specific tools to refresh cached images:
- Facebook: [Sharing Debugger](https://developers.facebook.com/tools/debug/)
- Twitter: [Card Validator](https://cards-dev.twitter.com/validator)
:::

::: tip
**Image dimensions matter:** Using recommended sizes ensures images display correctly without cropping or distortion. Smaller images may appear blurry, larger images will be resized and slow page load.
:::
