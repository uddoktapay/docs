# SEO Settings

Configure **Search Engine Optimization (SEO)** settings to improve your brand’s visibility on search engines and enhance how your content appears when shared on social platforms.

---

## Meta Description
Add a short, compelling description of your business.  
- **Usage:** Shown in search engine results below your site title.  
- **Recommendation:** Keep under **160 characters** for best display.

---

## SEO Keywords
Provide keywords that represent your business.  
- **Format:** Comma-separated list.  
- **Example:** `payments, invoices, uddoktapay, digital wallet`  

These help search engines understand your business focus.

---

## Social Media Sharing
Control how your brand appears when links are shared on platforms like Facebook, LinkedIn, and Twitter/X.  

- **Facebook/LinkedIn Image**  
  - Recommended size: **1200x630 px**  
  - Used as a preview image when your link is shared.  

- **Twitter/X Card Image**  
  - Recommended size: **1200x628 px**  
  - Appears in Twitter/X link previews.  

---

::: tip
Always upload high-quality images for social sharing to make your brand look professional across platforms.
:::
