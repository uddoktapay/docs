# General Settings

General settings allow you to configure your brand’s core information, currency, localization, analytics, and contact details. These settings define how your application behaves across reports, invoices, and customer-facing interfaces.

---

## Core Information

- **Site Name**  
  Name of your business or brand. This appears in the browser title and across different parts of the application.

- **Default Timezone**  
  Select the timezone for your business. All reports, invoices, and system times will follow this timezone.

---

## Currency & Financial

- **Default Currency**  
  Choose the default currency used in all transactions.  

- **Currency Symbol**  
  Auto-filled based on the selected currency (e.g., ৳ for BDT).  

::: warning
Changing the default currency affects all financial calculations. Configure carefully.
:::

---

## Localization

- **Default Language**  
  Select the default language for checkout page.  

- **Week Starts On**  
  Set the first day of the week (e.g., Saturday, Sunday, Monday). This affects how weekly reports are displayed.

---

## Analytics & Tracking

- **Google Tag Manager ID**  
  Enter your Tag Manager ID (e.g., `GTM-XXXXXXX`) to track customer activity and analytics.

---

## Business Details

Company information is used in **invoices and documentation**.

- **Street Address** — Address of your business.  
- **City/Town** — City where your business is located.  
- **Postal/ZIP Code** — Postal code for your region.  
- **Country** — Select your country of registration.  

---

## Contact & Support

Provide support contact details for your customers.

- **Support Phone Number** — With country code.  
- **Support Email Address** — Main email for customer inquiries.  
- **Support Website** — Link to your help center or portal.  

---

## Social Media Profiles

Link your official social media pages:

- **Facebook Page** — e.g., `https://facebook.com/yourcompany`  
- **Facebook Messenger** — e.g., `https://m.me/yourcompany`  
- **WhatsApp Number** — With country code.  
- **Telegram** — e.g., `https://t.me/yourcompany`  
- **YouTube Channel** — e.g., `https://youtube.com/@yourcompany`

---

::: tip
Keep your contact and social information updated so customers can reach you easily.
:::
