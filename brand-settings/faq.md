# FAQ

The FAQ section lets you publish answers to common questions that will appear on your brand’s **Checkout** page. This helps customers quickly find important information during payment.

---

## Overview

- **Where it appears:** FAQ button of Checkout page.  
- **Who sees it:** Your customers when they make a payment.  
- **Control:** You can add, edit, sort, enable or disable questions anytime.

::: warning
Only enabled FAQs are shown to customers. Disabled ones are hidden from Checkout.
:::

---

## Managing FAQs

The FAQ list shows each item’s **Title** and whether it’s **Active**.

- **Enable/Disable:** Use the switch to show or hide a question.  
- **Reorder:** Drag items up or down to change the order they appear.  
- **Edit/Delete:** Quickly update or remove an entry.  
- **Search:** Use the search box to find a question if your list is long.

---

## Adding a New FAQ

1. Click **New FAQ**.  
2. Fill in the form:  
   - **Title** — the customer’s question (e.g., *How long does payment verification take?*).  
   - **Content** — your answer.  
   - **Status** — leave enabled to show it right away.  
3. Click **Create**.

### Example

- **Title:** *How long does verification take?*  
- **Content:** *Most payments are verified instantly. Some banks may take up to 5 minutes.*  

---

## Editing FAQs

- Click **Edit** on an item to update the title, content, or status.  
- Changes take effect immediately on the Checkout page.

---

## Ordering FAQs

- Drag and drop to reorder your FAQs.  
- The order you set is the order shown to customers.

::: tip
Place the most common and important questions at the top (e.g., *How to pay*, *Verification time*, *Refund policy*).
:::

---

## Best Practices

- Keep questions short and answers clear.  
- Include only information that helps customers complete a payment.  
- Don’t publish sensitive details (like account numbers).  
- Link to your website or policy page if more detail is needed.  
- Review and update regularly to avoid outdated answers.

---

## Troubleshooting

- **FAQ not visible?**  
  Make sure the status is enabled.  

- **Wrong order?**  
  Check the drag-and-drop order and refresh the Checkout page.  

- **Typo or incorrect answer?**  
  Edit the entry and save your changes.  

::: tip
A few clear FAQs are better than many long ones. Keep it simple for your customers.
:::
