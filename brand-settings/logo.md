# Logo & Favicon

Manage the logos and favicon used throughout your application. These assets are shown in different areas such as the dashboard, checkout pages, emails, and browser tabs.

---

## Primary Logo
Upload your main brand logo.  
- **Usage:** Displayed in the dashboard header, customer checkout, and system emails.  
- **Recommended format:** PNG or SVG  
- **Recommended size:** At least **300px wide** for clear display.  

---

## Round Logo
Upload a round or square logo.  
- **Usage:** Used for avatars, profile pictures, and anywhere a compact version of your logo is needed.  
- **Recommended format:** PNG (transparent background preferred)  
- **Recommended size:** **100x100 px**  

---

## Favicon
Upload a favicon to personalize your browser tab.  
- **Usage:** Displayed in the browser tab, bookmarks, and shortcuts.  
- **Recommended format:** PNG or ICO  
- **Recommended size:** **32x32 px**  

---

::: tip
After uploading, click **Save changes** to apply your logos across the system.
:::
