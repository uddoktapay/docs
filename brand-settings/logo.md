## Introduction

Logo & Favicon settings allow you to upload and manage brand visuals that appear throughout your application, including headers, emails, browser tabs, and user interfaces.

Access Logo & Favicon from **Brand Settings → Logo & Favicon** to configure your brand identity visuals.

## Primary Logo

Main logo displayed in header and emails.

**Format:** JPG, JPEG, PNG  
**Recommended Size:** 250 × 70 pixels  

**Where it appears:**
- Email templates
- Invoice headers

**Upload area:** Rectangular box with drag & drop support.

::: tip
Use a transparent PNG for best results. Ensure your logo is clearly visible on both light and dark backgrounds.
:::

## Round Logo

Used for avatars and circular logo displays.

**Format:** JPG, JPEG, PNG  
**Recommended Size:** 512 × 512 pixels  

**Where it appears:**
- Checkout page
- Gateway page


::: tip
Use a square image (512×512) for best results in circular displays. The system automatically crops it to a circle.
:::

## Favicon

Browser tab icon appearing next to page title.

**Format:** JPG, JPEG, ICO, PNG  
**Recommended Size:** 32×32 pixels (displayed as "Browser tab icon (32×32 recommended)")  
**Accepted Size:** 64 × 64 pixels  

**Where it appears:**
- Browser tabs
- Bookmarks
- Browser history
- Address bar (on some browsers)


::: tip
ICO format provides best cross-browser compatibility. PNG works well for modern browsers. Keep the design simple and recognizable at small sizes.
:::
