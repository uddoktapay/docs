# Invoice Best Practices

- **Use clear descriptions**
  Make item descriptions specific so customers understand the bill.

- **Keep due dates realistic**
  Align due dates with your operational process and gateway settlement times.

- **Prefer “Cancel” over “Delete”**
  Canceled invoices remain visible for audit; deleted invoices are gone permanently.

- **Record payments with gateway and transaction ID**
  This ensures payments and invoices stay linked for accurate reporting.

- **Send reminders for unpaid invoices**
  Use the built-in reminder with variables and shortcodes for consistent communication.
