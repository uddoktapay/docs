# Invoice List

The list displays all invoices with key details like customer name, email, currency, amount, and status.

## Filters

Use the quick filters to view **All**, **Paid**, **Unpaid**, **Refunded**, or **Canceled**.

## Search

Search by customer name or email. Sorting is available on common columns.

## Row actions

- **Copy** — Duplicate the invoice with the same items and metadata.
- **View** — Open the invoice details.
- **Edit** — Modify items, amounts, due date, and other fields.
- **Delete** — Permanently remove the invoice.

::: warning
Deleting an invoice removes it from reporting and history. Consider **Cancel** if you need to keep the record without allowing payment.
:::
