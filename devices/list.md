# Device List

The device list shows all connected Android phones.

From here you can:

- **Search** devices by ID or name.  
- **Check status** (Connected or Disconnected).  
- **Run Balance Verification** for each device.  
- **Delete** devices that are no longer used.  

::: tip
Keep at least one device connected at all times to ensure real-time SMS capture.
:::
