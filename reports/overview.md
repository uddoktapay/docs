# Reports

The Reports section gives you a quick financial snapshot of your payments.  
You can view totals, counts, and success rates across common time periods.

## Available timeframes

- **Today**
- **Yesterday**
- **This Week / Last Week**
- **This Month / Last Month**
- **This Year / Last Year**
- **Custom Range** (pick start and end dates)

Each card shows:

- **Total amount received**
- **Number of payments completed**
- **Success percentage**
