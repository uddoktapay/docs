# Metrics

Each report card includes:

- **Total Amount** — sum of all completed payments in the period.
- **Payments Completed** — count of successful payments.
- **Success Rate** — percentage of attempted payments that were successful.

Example:


```

৳30.00
3 payments completed • 37.5% success

```

::: warning
If your success rate drops unexpectedly, check your gateways and failed payment logs.
:::
