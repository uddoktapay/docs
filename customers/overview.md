# Customers

The Customers module lets you maintain a directory of people or businesses who make payments through your system. Each customer record includes:

- Basic identity fields (name, email, phone)
- Optional address details (address, city, state, postcode, country)
- A unique record that links to their payments and invoices

Customers can be created manually, imported from a file, or updated directly in the system.
