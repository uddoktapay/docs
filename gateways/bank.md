# Bank Gateways

Bank Gateways are manual transfer methods. Customers pay by transferring funds directly to your bank account.

## Fields

Bank gateways share most fields with automatic gateways:

- **Gateway Name / Display Name**
- **Currency**
- **Minimum Amount / Maximum Amount**
- **Fixed Charge / Percentage Charge**

Additional required fields:

- **Bank Name**
- **Account Holder Name**
- **Account Number**
- **Branch Name**
- **Routing Number / SWIFT Code**
- **Logo and Description**

## User input fields

Admins can configure extra fields for the checkout form, e.g.:

- **Transaction ID (Trx ID)**
- **Proof of Payment upload**
- **Reference Number**

These fields ensure you collect the information needed to verify incoming transfers.

::: tip
Bank Gateways are best used when customers pay manually and proof must be verified by staff.
:::
