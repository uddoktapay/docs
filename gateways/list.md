# Gateways List

The list shows all gateways with display name, currency, and status. Use the toggle to enable or disable a gateway. Use **Edit** or **Delete** for maintenance.

A left sidebar groups gateways:

- **Automatic** — API, SDK, and MFS automations.
- **Bank Gateways** — Manual bank methods you define.

Search is available at the top. Pagination controls are at the bottom.
